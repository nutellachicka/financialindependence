Understanding My Expenses

Creating a budget

Necessary Expenses

Think of this category as planned expenses you incur every month. Things like:

  • Rent
  • Insurance
  • Debt
  • Utilities
  • Gas
  • Groceries

Discretionary Expenses

Think of this category as variable non-essential expenses that might be recurring or non-recurring. Things like:

  • Clothing
  • Gym
  • Entertainment
  • Restaurant
  • Hobbies
  • Personal Care

Excess Expenses

Think of this category as luxury expenses that are uncommon. Things like:

  • Charity
  • Gifts
  • Travel
  • Taxis
  • Luxury Items
